About NOVAC
NOVAC is a global collaboration between volcano observatories and research institutions to advance research and monitoring of volcanic emissions and its impact in the atmosphere.
Sustained By
The efforts of volcano observatories and: Chalmers University of Technology, the Volcano Disaster Assistance Program (VDAP), the European Commission, and the International Geoscience Programme of UNESCO/IUGS.
